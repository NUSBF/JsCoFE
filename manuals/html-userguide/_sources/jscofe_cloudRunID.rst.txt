===========
CloudRun ID
===========


Project page is the page you find yourself in immediately after login in your 
CCP4 Cloud account. Here you can find the Menu icon: |menu| in the top-left 
corner of the page. Click on it, then choose **"My Account"** from the dropdown 
menu, and you will be presented with your account settings. 

.. image:: images/project_page.png
         :scale: 50 %


.. image:: images/cloud_id.png
         :scale: 50 %
         :align: center

Please copy **CloudRun Id** from **"My Account"** to paste it into ccp4i 
(**2**) (**control-V**, **even on macOS**), and enter your login name (**1**).


.. image:: images/i1toCloud_5.png
         :scale: 50 %
         :align: center
