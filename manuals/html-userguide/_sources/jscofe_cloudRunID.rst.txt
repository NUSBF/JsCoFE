===========
CloudRun ID
===========

The Project page is the first page displayed after logging into your CCP4 Cloud account. On this page, you can locate the Menu icon: |menu| in the top-left corner. Click on it, then select **"My Account"** from the dropdown menu to access your account settings.

.. image:: images/project_page.png
         :scale: 50%

.. image:: images/cloud_id.png
         :scale: 50%
         :align: center

In the **"My Account"** section, copy your **CloudRun ID** and paste it into CCP4i (**2**) using **Control-V** (even on macOS). Additionally, enter your login name (**1**).

.. image:: images/i1toCloud_5.png
         :scale: 50%
         :align: center

Return to the `Cloud Run facility in CCP4i interface <./jscofe_i1toCloud.html>`_ article for further instructions.

        :width: 16
