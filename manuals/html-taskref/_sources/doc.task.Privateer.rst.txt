====================================================
Validation of carbohydrate structures with Privateer
====================================================

`Privateer <https://www.ccp4.ac.uk/html/privateer.html>`_ is a software package aimed at the detection and prevention of conformational, regiochemical, and stereochemical anomalies in cyclic monosaccharide structures. 

Privateer produces a visual checklist for rapid correction of the errors in real space and ensures that conformational preferences are accounted for during subsequent rebuilding and refinement.
