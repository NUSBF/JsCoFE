===============================
Prepare MR Ensemble from Models
===============================

**MR Ensembles** are superposed sets of sufficiently similar MR models the use of which often leads to a higher success rate comparing to the use of MR Search Models. This task form ensemble from the given models.

-----------
Trim levels
-----------

By default, the task will generate untrimmed ensemble and ensembles with 100%, 80%, 60%, and 40% best matching residue alignment. You may change the trim level by giving a comma-separated list of the trims.
