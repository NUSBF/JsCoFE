======
Phaser
======

`Phaser <https://journals.iucr.org/j/issues/2007/04/00/he5368/index.html>`_ is a program for phasing macromolecular crystal structures by both molecular replacement and experimental phasing methods.

Find out more about **Molecular Replacement with Phaser** you can `here <https://www.phaser.cimr.cam.ac.uk/index.php/Molecular_Replacement>`_ and about **Experimental Phasing with Phaser** by clicking `here <https://www.phaser.cimr.cam.ac.uk/index.php/Experimental_Phasing>`_

`Here <https://www.phaser.cimr.cam.ac.uk/index.php/Top_Ten_Tips>`_ ten tips in for phasing in Phaser

For more visit `PhaserWiki <https://www.phaser.cimr.cam.ac.uk/index.php/Phaser_Crystallographic_Software>`_
