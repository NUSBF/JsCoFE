5.1. Morda¶

MoRDa is a pipeline for automated molecular replacement protein structure solution based on its own domain database which derived from the PDB and contains a compact description of non-redundant protein chains, domains, homo- and hetero-oligomers. Models relevant to the target sequence are further adjusted before molecular replacement search.The domain models are preprocessed to exclude flexible loops, which may hinder the molecular replacement search.
