12.2. Validation of carbohydrate structures with Privateer¶

Privateer is a software package aimed at the detection and prevention of conformational, regiochemical, and stereochemical anomalies in cyclic monosaccharide structures.

Privateer produces a visual checklist for rapid correction of the errors in real space and ensures that conformational preferences are accounted for during subsequent rebuilding and refinement.
