6.1.6. Prepare MR Ensemble from Sequence¶

MR Ensembles are superposed sets of sufficiently similar MR models, corresponding to the same target sequence. Using ensembles instead of singe models often increases chances of successful Molecular Replacement. This task uses MrBump software to prepare MR Ensembles for given target sequence. The process is completely automatic and includes finding suitable structure homologs in the PDB and, optionally, AlphaFold 2 database, trimming them and bringing in best superposition.

The task may prepare several MR Ensembles up to the specified number, depending on the quality of models found.
